01-usgs-station-acquire.py

Creates CSV files for all USGS water quality stations.

usage: 01-usgs-station-acquire.py [-h] [--get-instantaneous] [--write-to-csv]
                                  [--index-start INDEX_START]

Named Arguments

--get-instantaneous

Get instantaneous flow data or modeled continuous data.

Default: False

--write-to-csv

Write out CSVs to data/ on the local machine?

Default: False

--index-start

Array indexing (0-based or 1-based)

Default: 0
